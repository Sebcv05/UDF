## Critical Bug Fixes Applied

### 1. ✅ Bubble Density Calculation (Nov 9, 2025)
**Problem:** Used safe_divide() with wrong threshold (1e-20) for nanometer bubbles
**Fix:** Direct calculation `rho_v = m_b / Vb` with floor at 1e-6 kg/m³
**Impact:** Bubbles now grow correctly from nucleation

### 2. ✅ Antoine Coefficients (Nov 12, 2025)
**Problem:** Wrong coefficients gave 20× too high P_sat
**Fix:** Corrected to A=6.67956, B=1002.711, C=25.215 (from euler_explicit.py)
**Impact:** Realistic superheat levels and growth rates

### 3. ✅ Dual Geometry Heat Transfer (Nov 12, 2025)
**Problem:** Only used semi-infinite formulation
**Fix:** Added film formulation, taking max(Q_si, Q_film)
**Impact:** Proper heat transfer as bubble approaches droplet surface

### 4. ✅ Evaporation Interaction (Nov 9, 2025)
**Problem:** Standard evaporation cooled droplets too fast
**Fix:** Skip evaporation for parent parcels (is_child == 0)
**Impact:** Thermal breakup can proceed without interference

## Physics Summary

### Bubble Growth (RPE)
1. **Initialization:** R₀ = 1.1×Rc when parcel enters thermal breakup
2. **Evolution:** Explicit Euler with dt = 100 ps
3. **Heat transfer:** max(semi-infinite, film) for all bubble sizes
4. **Mass transfer:** Thermal-limited mdot = Q/L_v
5. **Pressure:** Ideal gas P_b = ρ_v × R_spec × T
6. **Dynamics:** Full Rayleigh-Plesset with inertial terms

### Thermal Breakup
1. **Criterion:** kb = η/δ > 1 (DGRE)
2. **Trigger:** Creates N_child = 12 parcels
3. **Size distribution:** Rosin-Rammler with n = 3.2
4. **Conservation:** Exact mass and D₃₂
